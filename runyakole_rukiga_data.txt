--- Runyakole/Rukiga Language Knowledge Base ---

Greetings:
- Hello (singular): Oraire ota
- Hello (plural): Muraire muta
- How are you? (singular): Oli ota?
- How are you? (plural): Muli muta?
- I am fine: Ndi kurungi
- Welcome: Murakaza nungi

Basic Vocabulary:
- Person: Omuntu (singular), Abantu (plural)
- Child: Omwana (singular), Abaana (plural)
- Water: Amaizi
- Food: Ebyokurya
- House: Enyumba

Grammar Notes:
- Noun Classes: Runyakole/Rukiga has several noun classes indicated by prefixes.
  - 'Om-'/'Ab-': Used for people. E.g., Omuntu (person), Abantu (people).
  - 'Eki-'/'Ebi-': Used for things. E.g., Ekintu (thing), Ebintu (things).

Example Sentences:
- Omuntu oli ota? (Person, how are you?)
- Abaana bali kurungi. (The children are fine.)
